About Us

Welcome

Computer science forms the cornerstone of the contemporary information technology era. Consequently, for humanity to achieve rapid economic progress and fully capitalize on information technology, a foundational understanding of computer science is imperative. This encompasses the exploration and development of programs facilitating daily computer usage, the underlying software systems supporting these applications, and the methodologies and protocols employed in crafting applications and systems that operate reliably, accurately, and ethically. Central to computer science is the proficiency in programming computers. Employers seeking computer scientists prioritize individuals skilled in programming, well-versed in current methodologies and tools, and capable of applying sound engineering principles to create, build, and maintain computer programs.

Our alumni are equipped for the field of computing through a range of departmental courses and the skills they develop. These courses empower students to collaborate in a project team, engage in research and development, or excel in other environments where their specialized skills can contribute significantly. Furthermore, the program provides students exposure to contemporary research topics in computer science, along with both existing and emerging technologies and methodologies, creating a foundation for further study or research.

The Department of Computer Science offers two undergraduate degree programs: the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology and the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1).
